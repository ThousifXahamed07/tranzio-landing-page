Questions

Good to know.

What is Tranzio?

Tranzio is a mobility media network. We fit auto-rickshaws, cabs, and buses with smart digital screens, so brands can run hyperlocal video campaigns in front of commuters — and drivers earn from every journey.

How does advertising on Tranzio work?

You choose an area, a time window, and a budget. Your campaign plays on screens inside vehicles moving through that area — geofenced to your neighbourhood and timed to your customers' day. Every play is logged with proof-of-play telemetry, so you only pay for what actually ran.

What do drivers and fleet partners earn?

Drivers earn a fixed monthly payout of around ₹2,500–₹3,000 for keeping the screen active — with zero effort and no change to how they work. Fleet operators earn across their whole fleet.

When and where does Tranzio launch?

Our pilot launches in Bengaluru in 2026, starting with tech and student hubs like Koramangala, HSR Layout, and Bellandur — then expanding across Karnataka and to more cities.

How do I get started?

Brands can book a demo at sales@tranzioindia.com. Drivers and fleet operators can write to partner@tranzioindia.com. For everything else, reach us at hello@tranzioindia.com.
